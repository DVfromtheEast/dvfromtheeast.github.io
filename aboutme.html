About me

Hello! My name is Duc Vuong, or you can just call me DV.

I’m a design generalist from Vietnam. I enjoy designing for brands, digital products and print matters. I also work with plain HTML, CSS, JS or with CMS. Beside that, I have experience in creating web layout for dynamic websites by framework like Next.js using library Material UI.

I can quickly adapt to new projects and integrate with coworkers, optimize work processes to solve problems effectively.

I have graduated from Hanoi University of Industrial Fine Art with a Bachelor in Industrial and Product Design. Initially I have earned 5 years experience in branding design, print design and illustration during working in agencies and start-ups. In early 2018, I had my interest in Game Concept Art and Game User Interface when I joined a mobile game studio. During that time I had the opportunity to work on Mobile Management App projects. I constantly focused on UI/UX Research and UI Design and I had learned myself Material Design and Human Interface Guidelines. After that I switch completely to working on designing UI/UX in a SAAS product company. To me, each product has its own important role and can affect significantly to a specific aspect of human ‘s life.

I have learned HTML and CSS in order to have deeper understanding of the front-end development process, I can view the problems from different vantage points. Therefore I know how to make designing and coding works to be more efficient together and have less conflict.

Today, my main focus is helping clients in making brands, website and other digital products & services.

If you have a project you want to discuss, feel free to Get In Touch with me and we can find the best way to help you to take your project forward.
