Uranium

Web - Desktop & Mobile

Uranium Finance is a crypto token. Uranium Finance provides a decentralized financial asset that rewards users through financial protocol. The keys for its landing page are the simplicity and convenience, offering users a smooth experience without overloading and complicated data visual.

Role: UI/UX designer

Timeline: 2 months

Tools: Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

Result

The webpage has been designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind. With a dark background and few gentle, abstract graphic elements, The site has a modern and stylish look. Being easy to navigate, and it's also simple enough for people who aren't familiar with cryptocurrencies to understand what they're looking at.

Popup Overlay

The design of the popup also follows the principle of simplicity but still stands out enough with a subtle abstract pattern in the background.

Sub-pages

Stay consistent with the design system. Minimal illustrations and colors were used in a reasonable way to make a great experience.
