Mmenu

Web - Desktop & Mobile

Mmenu is a software development company that provides digital solutions and devices to the merchants in the F&B industry. This brand needs to showcase to a very particular audience - the business owners, to emphasize personality, pros and capacity of its products.

Role: UI/UX designer, Photo Editer, Frontend Dev

Timeline: 2 months

Tools: Figma, Squarespace, Photoshop

Result

The website is focused on attracting the business owners who are willing to apply technology to their operation system in order to cut loss and optimize the benefit, so the design should support that idea. Brand color, round shapes, enticing images and clean typeface create an attractive and peaceful environment that facilitates learning about the brand and its products.
